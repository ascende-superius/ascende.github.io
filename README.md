# Privacy Policy for Active Recall Companion

**Effective Date:** December 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Duc Truong Huu ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in the Active Recall Companion mobile application (the "App").

**This is a template and is not legal advice. You should consult a legal professional to ensure your policy is appropriate for your app and compliant with all laws.**

### Information We Do Not Collect

We believe in privacy by design. The Active Recall Companion app **does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personally identifiable information**.

*   **No User Accounts:** The App does not require you to create an account.
*   **No Analytics:** The App does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.
*   **No Advertising:** The App does not contain any third-party advertisements.

### Information You Create (Local Data)

All the data you create in the App, such as flashcards, topics, and study progress, is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's Core Data framework. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

### iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your Apple account, your Core Data may be automatically synced across your devices. This process is managed entirely by Apple and your private iCloud account. We do not have access to any data stored in your iCloud account.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: **thduc.dn@gmail.com**

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
